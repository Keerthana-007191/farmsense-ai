

# FarmSense: AI-Powered Agriculture Platform Website

## Overview
A modern, investor-ready single-page website for FarmSense — an AI-powered crop monitoring platform that combines acoustic sensing and computer vision to detect pests and diseases early.

## Design System
- **Color Palette**: Rich greens (#16a34a, #15803d), white, soft green-to-white gradients, dark accents for contrast
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif with bold headlines and readable body text
- **Style**: Minimal, spacious layout with generous whitespace, soft shadows, and rounded cards
- **Animations**: Fade-in on scroll, hover scale effects on cards, smooth transitions

## Sections

### 1. Sticky Navigation Bar
- FarmSense wordmark on the left
- Nav links: About, Features, How It Works, Technology, Contact
- "Request Demo" CTA button
- Backdrop blur effect on scroll

### 2. Hero Section
- Bold headline: "Protect Your Crops with Intelligent AI Monitoring"
- Subtext about acoustic sensing and computer vision
- Two CTA buttons: "Request Demo" (green) and "Explore FarmSense" (outlined)
- Green gradient background with subtle pattern/overlay
- Fade-in animation on load

### 3. About FarmSense
- Problem statement about delayed pest/disease detection
- FarmSense as "24/7 intelligent farm guardian"
- Two-column layout with text and visual icon element

### 4. Features Section (5 Icon Cards)
- Acoustic Pest Detection
- AI-Powered Crop Disease Recognition
- Real-Time Mobile Alerts
- Smart Treatment Recommendations
- Multi-Modal AI (Sound + Vision)
- Cards with Lucide icons, soft shadows, hover scale animation

### 5. How It Works (4-Step Flow)
- Horizontal steps with connecting lines/arrows
- Sensor & Camera → AI Processing → Threat Detection → Instant Alerts
- Each step with icon and label

### 6. Benefits Grid
- 5 items: Early warning, Reduced crop loss, Saves labor/time, Data-driven decisions, Scalable & cost-effective
- Grid layout with icons and descriptions

### 7. Technology Stack
- Dark/gradient background section
- Badge/pill components for: AI, ML, Computer Vision, Acoustic Sensors, Edge AI
- Tagline: "Built with next-generation AI to power the future of agriculture."

### 8. Future Vision
- Inspiring paragraph about autonomous AI farm assistant
- Large typography, quote-style layout

### 9. Contact / Demo Form
- Form fields: Name, Email, Organization, Message
- CTA: "Book a Demo and Bring Intelligence to Your Fields"
- Toast notification on submission

### 10. Footer
- FarmSense branding, copyright, quick nav links
- Minimal, clean design

## Technical Approach
- Single-page React app with smooth scroll navigation between sections
- Fully responsive mobile/tablet/desktop
- No backend — form shows success toast on submit
- Intersection Observer for scroll-triggered fade-in animations
- Lucide icons throughout
- Green-themed CSS variables in the design system
